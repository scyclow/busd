About

bUSD is an ERC20 token issued on Ethereum L1. It will only be issued during an official Burn Ceremony, which involves burning real US Dollar notes.

bUSD is not a stablecoin It can never be redeemed for USD, and may trade at values other than $1 for extended periods of time.

bUSD is not a security There is no utility or reasonable expectation of profit from owning it.

bUSD is not legal tender It is issued by the Burn Agent, who has no affliation with the US Government.

Burning US currency is a federal crime punishable for up to six months in prison.

See the Burn Agreement for more information.
